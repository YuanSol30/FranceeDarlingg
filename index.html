Before I fully let go

This page is for the memories, the lessons, the apology, and the quiet goodbye.

One last message Since 2024

I read everything you said, and I understand. I know I hurt you many times, and I won’t deny any of it. I’m really sorry for everything I did. Hindi ko na ijujustify yung mga pagkakamali ko, because I know I failed you and I became someone you said you never wanted. But before I fully let go, I just want to say this one last time, and I hope you read it kahit once lang.

I realized something about myself, lablab. I was so focused on myself, my fears, my insecurities, my jealousy, and my habits that hurt you and this relationship. Hindi yan fair as in, never siyang patas. You gave me your time, your patience, and your heart, and I didn’t do my job the way I should have. Instead of making you feel safe and loved, I made you tired, hurt, and disappointed, and I’m sorry for that.

The moment we met last 2024 was really special to me. It didn’t feel random to me. It felt like the start of something important in my life. I still remember our first talks, the jokes, the nights we stayed up, the games we played, and the small things that made me happy. From the start, I was already so invested in you.

I remember our video calls, solving Rubik’s cube together, you teaching me better combinations, and even the little things I overthought. Looking back, I know I made mistakes and I probably made you feel controlled or pressured, and I’m really sorry for that. I remember the times I failed to protect your feelings, and until now I regret that so much.

I remember how you could lose your mood for the simplest reasons and how sometimes it was just because you were hungry. I found those small things cute. I remember wanting to take care of you in little ways, even when things became harder. When you agreed to let me court you, I was genuinely so happy. That was one of the happiest moments for me.

When I found out I would finally see you, I was so excited that I couldn’t even sleep properly. I was nervous and overthinking everything, even how I would look in front of you. When I finally saw you in person, sobrang ganda mo talaga. I will never forget that moment. You gave me confidence to be less shy, and those moments became some of the best memories of my life.

I remember everything we did together, the simple places we went, the pictures, the food, the flowers, the necklace, the first experiences, and the feeling that I was lucky because it was you. I wished we lived closer, so I could see you more, take you out, sit with you at night under the sky, and just be with you.

I remember so much about you, not only the memories but who you are. I remember your humor, your voice, your smile, your eyes, the things you love, the colors you like, the food you enjoy, the games, the night sky, roses, and the dreams you talked about. I also remember the things you hated, especially dishonesty, irresponsibility, disrespect, and people who do not make you feel safe. It hurts me to admit that I became someone who made you feel some of those things, someone I promised I would never be.

I remember the rules we talked about, never cheating, never lying, always communicating, respecting each other, always checking up, and never sleeping while we were fighting. I remember the promises we made, that it would only be us. I know I broke some of those, and that is why we are here now.

I still look back at our videos, our monthsary memories, and everything we built together. For me, none of it was fake. I truly loved you, and part of me still does. I know sorry isn’t enough, and I know I don’t deserve another chance right now. I’m not going to force you or pressure you anymore. If this is really the end for you, I’ll respect your decision and I’ll stop contacting you like you asked.

But if there’s even a small chance someday, even if it is not now, I hope you can remember me and our memories without pain. And even if I’m no longer part of your life, I sincerely wish you the best. I hope life treats you kindly. I hope you become successful, peaceful, happy, and surrounded by people who truly love and protect you.

I hope you get to experience all the beautiful things you deserve, and I hope you never lose the light, confidence, and softness that made you so special to me. Thank you for everything, for your time, your love, your presence, and for being one of the best things that ever happened to me. I’m really sorry for everything, and I’ll always be thankful for you.

Thank you for the time
Sorry for the hurt
Goodbye with respect
